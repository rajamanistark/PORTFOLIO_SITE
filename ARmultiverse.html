Project Description

AR Multiverse is a cutting-edge augmented reality (AR) application that revolutionizes the construction industry. It seamlessly integrates virtual 3D models into real-world environments, providing an immersive and realistic visualization experience. With cloud-based collaboration and real-time data synchronization, stakeholders can easily collaborate and make informed decisions. Advanced features like the measuring tape module and annotation tools enhance accuracy and communication. AR Multiverse prioritizes safety by identifying potential hazards and clashes, minimizing risks. It transforms the way construction projects are visualized and executed, empowering stakeholders to work more efficiently, improve coordination, and reduce costly rework.
